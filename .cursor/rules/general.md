## Communication

- Prefer concise, high-signal explanations.
- When reporting errors, include the exact failing file/function and the user-facing message.

## Engineering

- Prefer small, incremental changes with a quick smoke-test.
- Do not add large dependencies unless needed.
- Avoid hardcoding absolute paths; resolve paths relative to the module when possible.
